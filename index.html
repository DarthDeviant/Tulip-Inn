About Us

A Peaceful Retreat in the Heart of Assam

Tulip Inn Homestay offers a comfortable and peaceful stay with a perfect blend of modern convenience and homely warmth. Nestled in the lush landscapes of Harmutty, North Lakhimpur, the homestay features clean, well-furnished rooms, a calm atmosphere, and essential amenities to ensure a truly relaxing experience.

Guests can enjoy privacy, attentive hospitality, and a welcoming environment that makes them feel at home. Ideal for families, couples, and solo travelers, Tulip Inn Homestay is a great choice for both short visits and extended stays — surrounded by the natural beauty of upper Assam.
